About Us:

The main aim of "Apni Madaad App" is to provide the basic living previleges(shoes ,clothes etc) to those who need it more then any one. This idea was developed by a group of four students of Ned University of Engineering and Technology to go a step further and to help the society and to play that part in the society that is not done by many.Through this Application people who wants to take a step in helping the needy could be provided an easy plateform to do so.
